Roland DG Fab12

Event Introduction

Roland DG is the sub-brand of the music instrument brand, Roland.
It is a digital fabrication machine maker producing 3D printers, CNCs and large-format printers.
Fab12 was the 12th annual event hosted by MIT.
Roland DG had a booth in Fab12 to display their machines targeting digital makers.
