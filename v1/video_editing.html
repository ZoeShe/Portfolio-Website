Video Editing Work Samples

TECHNICAL TOOLS: Adobe Premiere | Final Cut Pro

Motion Graphics

Motion graphic samples made for 45R's online/offline events. These contents were used on the website, instagram posts and weekly newsletters. I laid the elements on the floor and shot the graphics with iPhoneX in a small room. I edited the graphics for each frame with photoshop and created the motion video with Final Cut Pro.

Promotion Video

Promotion video made for Roland DG China for the product exhibition in Shengzhen, China. I collected video records from the cameraman and made the video to promote the brand and products on YouTube and some Chinese local medias. The video production was done with Adobe Premiere.
