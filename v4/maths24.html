Maths24

Maths24 is an old Shanghainese poker game played since 1960. The goal is to get the result of 24 by operating 4 integers with elementary operators. J, Q and K stand for number 11, 12 and 13. Each card must be used once and only once. The game ends when the deck of cards is dealt.
